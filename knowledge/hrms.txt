HRMS for ERPNext/Frappe typically supports:
- Employee lifecycle management
- Leave and attendance
- Shift assignment
- Payroll processing
- Expense claims
- Performance management (depending on setup)

Discovery questions to ask prospects:
- Number of employees/users
- Payroll complexity and statutory requirements
- Attendance source (manual/biometric/device integration)
- Existing HR/payroll system and migration needs
- Approval hierarchy and policy requirements

Implementation checkpoints:
- Employee master data quality
- Leave policy design
- Salary structure and components
- Payroll calendar and test runs
- Access control by HR role and department
